MUSEUMS IN NUR-SULTAN

National Museum of the Republic of Kazakhstan

The National Museum of the Republic of Kazakhstan was opened on July 9, 2015. It is the largest museum in the country.

          Location: Tauelsizdik 54
          Schedule: 10.00-18.00
          Contact: 69-30-41

Museum of the First President of the Republic of Kazakhstan body

The Museum of the First President of the Republic of Kazakhstan is a museum in Nur-Sultan
dedicated to the First President of the Republic of Kazakhstan,
Nursultan Nazarbayev.

            Location: Alikhan Bokeikhan 1a
            Schedule: 10.00-18.00
            Contact: 69-30-41

Saken Seifullin Museum

The Saken Seifullin Museum is a museum in the city of Nur-Sultan dedicated to the life and work of the Kazakh statesman, poet and writer Saken Seifullin. It is located in a building of the XIX century, which is included in the list of historical and cultural monuments of local significance

            Location: M.Auezov 20
            Schedule: 10.00-18.00
            Contact: 69-30-41

Museum of Modern Art

One of the most visited museums of the capital of Kazakhstan, Astana, is the Museum of Modern Art. The opening of the museum took place in October 1980 and was timed to coincide with the 25th anniversary of the development of virgin and fallow lands.

            Location: Alikhan Bokeikhan 1a
            Schedule: 10.00-18.00
            Contact: 69-30-41

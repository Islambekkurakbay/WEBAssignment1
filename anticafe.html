Anti-cafe



is a new and very affordable form of entertainment, where in the company of friends and relatives you can have fun,
have fun, and at the same time have a good time, while feeling homely. We present you a list of creative institutions in Astana.

Eagilik–Books& coffee

Calm atmosphere and wonderful desserts. Here you can play with friends over a cup of fruit tea, spend time at work, and also buy a book in several languages, because there is a library on the territory of the cafe. Membership in the library provides access to a free conversation club with a native English speaker.

Average bill: 1500 tenge
Working hours: Monday-Saturday from 10:00-21:00
Address: 61/1 Kenesary Street
Contacts: 8 702 682 28 77

Meowcatcafe

The Cat-cafe format is gaining popularity. Coffee, sweets and cute cats - is it really necessary for the happiness of a cat lover? They say that cats relieve stress and negative energy, as well as lift the mood. Meow cat cafe provides an opportunity to play board games and have a cup of tea in the company of tailed friends.

Cost: adults - 2000 tenge per hour, children (under 16 years old) - 1500 tenge per hour
Working hours: from 12:00 to 21:00. On weekends, recording is conducted from 10:00
Address: Komarova str., 27
Contacts: 8 708 707 00 55

Оyinn

Homely and warm atmosphere, seasoned with its special design. A great place to spend time with friends. Here you will find a variety of games and branded tea. Monopoly, Katan, Twister, Chess, Ekivoki, table tennis, football, PS4, hammocks for recreation, and the list of entertainment does not end there. Have fun and take photos in the local restroom, there you will find an unusual mirror.

Cost: 800 tenge per hour per person, unlimited - 2500 tenge per person (common room). VIP room 3000 tenge per person, unlimited. Rent for at least 8 people
Working hours: from 12:00 to 22:00
Address: 24 Amanzholova str.
Contacts: 8 777 666 09 49

Dream&Do

This institution is suitable for lovers of spiritual practices, transformational games, lectures and seminars. Here you can buy a wish card, play mafia, take a seminar on stylistics and watch movies in the company, taste delicious and fragrant coffee.

Working hours: from 10:00 to 22:00. The cafe can be visited by appointment
Address: 21 A.Khrapaty str. (Barcelona residential complex)
Contacts: 8 778 244 66 88

Deja Vu

Birthday is a very significant holiday, especially for children. Children, like their parents, want it to be remembered for a long time. In Deja Vu you will find everything for a bright holiday, themed parties, graduations and master classes are held here. Bring your game, invite animators, play the sensational "grope"

Cost:  35,000 tenge (3 hours)
Address: 39 Mangilik El Ave.
Contacts: 8 708 717 95 69

Некафе

With its atmosphere, this place unites many people, and they all have one goal – to have a good rest. You only pay for time, but you get a lot of nice bonuses: popular board games, new acquaintances, a variety of delicious tea, movies on the projector. Birthday parties, barbecue gatherings and master classes are held here.

Cost: 600 tenge per hour per person, 3000 tenge per person - unlimited
Working hours: from 12:00
Address: Gabdullina str., 17/1
Contacts: 8 700 070 70 71

Candy club

Perfect for family holidays. A large number of animators, a variety of bright costumes, photo shoots, game rooms, light music will perfectly complement a child's birthday.

Cost: starting from 65,000 tenge (3 hours)
Working hours: from 10:00 to 22:00
Address: Kaysenova str., 4 (Arnau residential complex)
Contacts: 8 747 250 05 17

Один к одному

This institution does not fit into the anti-cafe format, but it can be attributed to creative institutions. Here you can take a picture against the background of anime characters, taste Japanese beer under the anime Hayao Miyazaki.

Average bill: 4000 tenge
Working hours: from 10:00 to 23:00
Address: A.Mambetova str., 16
